What is a vulnerability?

A vulnerability is a technical issue with a government website or domain that attackers or hackers could use to exploit the service and its users. Unless an organisation's vulnerability disclosure programme explicitly mentions payment, you will not be paid a reward for reporting a vulnerability (known as a "bug bounty").
